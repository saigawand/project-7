Programmes in RV University

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMMES

Our courses have been curated keeping in mind the latest industrial practices and innovations, providing a blend of liberal arts and professional degrees. We make the most out of the innovative technologies and learning environment to teach skills that are valued by the employers.

We offer a wide range of undergraduate courses in Bangalore across our three schools to students who want to pursue B.Sc. (Hons.), B.A. (Hons.), B. Des., B.B.A. (Hons.), B.Com (Hons.), and B.A. (Hons.) (Economics).
